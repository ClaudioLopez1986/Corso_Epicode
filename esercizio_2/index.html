Focaccia with Caramalized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vingar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dought ingredinets until the flour is fully absorded. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimers knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
